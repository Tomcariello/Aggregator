About

Created as a collaborative project by Tom Cariello, Aanchal (Annie) Gupta, Melissa Baldwin, and Jesse Happel. Using the SeatGeek and JsonOdds APIs, The Sports Aggregator provides sports betting fanatics with reliable odds for a full variety of sports events along with easy access to tickets to get to the game!
